NBR Hair Extensions

Natural Beaded Row extensions.

Our specialty service. No heat, no glue, no adhesives — custom-colored wefts installed on hand-tied beaded rows. Every package includes color blending and a finished blowout. Maintenance every 6–8 weeks; extensions last 6 months to a year.

Polish · 1 Row

18″ · 22″ available

Perfect for women seeking added volume and texture in their existing natural or slightly longer hair.
