A Private, Elevated Experience

Where Confidence Meets Compassion

Hair loss can feel overwhelming, whether it’s caused by medical treatment, hormonal changes, stress, or genetics. At Studio One Hair Design, we’ve created a private, elevated experience where you can breathe, feel supported, and explore solutions that bring your confidence back without pressure, judgment, or confusion.

Here, you’re not “buying a wig.” You’re reclaiming the way you want to feel every single day.

Your First Appointment

Your journey begins with a one-on-one appointment designed to be relaxed, informative, and empowering.

In our private fitting space, you’ll receive:

  • A warm welcome and an open conversation
  • A gentle scalp & density evaluation
  • Cap sizing and precise measurements
  • Length, density & style recommendations
  • Overview of medical-grade options
  • Insurance guidance
  • Transparent next steps + timelines
