Frequently Asked Hair Extensions Questions:

ISLA™ Hair is only sold to artists who are NBR® Certified. To preserve the integrity and quality of the hair extensions, only those who have undergone the proper training to correctly apply the wefts using the NBR® Method can purchase and use these hair extensions. While numerous hair brands assert the use of solely human hair, they often incorporate synthetic fibers and silicone coatings. Although these additives may provide a temporary illusion of silkiness and thickness, they are susceptible to damage from heat and chemicals, resulting in accelerated wear and tear. ISLA™ distinguishes itself by exclusively utilizing Remy 100% human hair, renowned for its exceptional longevity and superior quality. ISLA™ Hand-Tied hair, utilized in the NBR® method, is a weft-type of Remy hair. “Remy” denotes that all strands face the same direction, which minimizes matting and tangling, ensuring a seamless flow. Renowned for its exceptional quality, the Remy hairline boasts a longer lifespan compared to other brands. Highly coveted by industry professionals, it is exclusively available to NBR® certified artists. Hand-tied extensions cover more surface area on your head, which results is a natural and seamless look. When we professionally apply the extensions, they are impossible to tell apart from your natural hair.
