Why NBR at Studio One

A method built for fullness, length, and hair health.

Studio One specializes in Natural Beaded Row hand-tied hair extensions using ISLA Hair, available only to NBR Certified artists. No heat, no glue, no adhesives — just beaded rows designed for minimal damage and maximum wear.

1

Certified Artists

ISLA Hair is only available to NBR Certified stylists. Every row at Studio One is installed by a specialist.

2

No Damage Method

Installation uses beaded rows with no heat, no glue, and no adhesives — keeping your natural hair intact.

3

Custom Blended

Extensions are color matched and blended in-house for a result that looks and moves like your own hair.

Designed to Last

Move-ups every 6–8 weeks keep your install fresh. Each row is engineered for long-term wear.
