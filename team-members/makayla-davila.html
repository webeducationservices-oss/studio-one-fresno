Makayla Davila

Hair Designer

I'm a hair designer who loves helping people feel confident and beautiful. I specialize in creating effortless, confidence-boosting hair through custom color, healthy hair care, and natural-looking extensions. I'm passionate about continuing to refine my craft and staying inspired by new techniques that help my guests feel their absolute best.

When I'm not behind the chair, you'll usually find me staying active whether it's playing pickleball, exploring the coast, or spending quality time with my family. I also love the creative side of life, from sewing and thrifting unique finds to playing board games and traveling to new places.

My goal is to provide every guest with a welcoming, elevated, and professional experience while helping them achieve strong, healthy, beautiful hair they love.

Specialties

  • Custom color
  • Healthy hair care
  • Natural-looking extensions
