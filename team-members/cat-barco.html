Studio One Team

Cat Barco

Owner & Hair Designer

With nearly three decades behind the chair, Cat has built her career in Fresno, California, helping women feel confident and beautiful. Her specialty is creating natural-looking, custom-blended beaded row extensions and dimensional color that transform looks while supporting hair health.

As a mom of three and empty nester, Cat connects deeply with her guests. She provides low-maintenance, high-impact solutions that make women feel like the best version of themselves.

Trust is foundational to her work. Cat approaches extensions as seamless enhancements rather than add-ons, and maintains commitment to education for herself and mentored stylists. Beyond the salon, she enjoys travel, sports, podcasts, and escaping to Las Vegas with her husband to reset.

At Studio One, every detail aims to make guests feel welcomed and celebrated. Cat's commitment is providing exceptional service in a professional yet warm environment, using techniques and products for strong, healthy, beautiful hair.

Specialties

  • Natural-looking beaded row extensions
  • Custom-blended extensions
  • Dimensional color
  • Low-maintenance hair solutions
