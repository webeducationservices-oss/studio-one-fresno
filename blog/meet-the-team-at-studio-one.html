Meet the Team at Studio One

Studio One September 1, 2025

At Studio One, we believe a salon is only as special as the people who make it thrive. Our team is more than just a group of stylists—we're educators, creatives, and confidence-builders who care deeply about every guest who sits in our chairs. Each member brings a unique skill set, passion, and personality to the salon, making Studio One a place where beauty, artistry, and connection come together.

Carina specializes in flawless gray coverage, subtle highlights, and seamless extensions. She creates looks that feel natural and polished, ensuring guests leave with confidence that shines from within.

Hope combines creativity with compassion, specializing in color services, perms, and personalized hair loss solutions. She understands that hair is deeply personal and takes time to ensure guests feel supported through every life stage.

Makayla has a gift for making hair look and feel naturally beautiful. Her specialties include gray coverage, soft highlights, Brazilian keratin treatments, and extensions. Guests love her ability to create consistent results that bring shine and smoothness.

Amber brings years of experience and training alongside one of Vidal Sassoon's top artists. She specializes in balayage, dimensional color, and precision haircuts, focusing on shape, balance, and movement.

Cat is the owner of Studio One with nearly 30 years behind the chair. She specializes in extensions and custom color that feel polished and natural. Known for her expertise in creating results that save women time while boosting confidence, Cat has built Studio One into a salon celebrating artistry, innovation, and community.

The best part is you can explore each stylist's specialties and find the perfect fit for your hair journey. Ready to meet our team in person? Book your appointment now and see everyone's availability online.
