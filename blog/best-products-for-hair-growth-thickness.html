Best Products for Hair Growth & Thickness

Studio One Team October 27, 2025

Can Thinning Hair Be Reversed?

If your once-thick ponytail now feels smaller or you've noticed more scalp visibility, you're not alone. Thinning hair is one of the most common beauty concerns women face. At Studio One Hair Design, we want you to know that thinning hair can be improved with the right plan, consistency, and professional guidance.

While some causes of hair loss are temporary like stress, hormones, or postpartum changes, others require a long-term approach focused on scalp health, internal nutrition, and commitment. Professional-grade products like Nutrafol and Oribe's Serene Scalp Densifying Line, combined with education and personalized care, help clients see progress from root to tip.

How Can I Stimulate Hair Growth?

Healthy hair begins with a healthy scalp. Think of it like planting in fertile soil—your roots need nourishment, moisture, and stimulation to thrive.

Ways to naturally and professionally stimulate new growth include scalp massage using gentle pressure or a scalp brush to increase circulation and wake up dormant follicles. Oribe Serene Scalp Densifying Treatment uses caffeine, biotin, and peptides to energize the scalp and support thicker, fuller hair. Professional treatments customized to detoxify and balance the scalp are available through our menu of services. Hair growth thrives when you drink plenty of water, manage stress, and eat nutrient-dense foods rich in protein and minerals.

For immediate confidence while growing hair back, consider beaded row hair extensions, available at Studio One Fresno. They blend seamlessly and protect natural hair while it strengthens underneath.

What Vitamin Am I Lacking If My Hair Is Thinning?

Hair thinning often signals missing key nutrients. Nutrafol targets deficiencies with vitamins, minerals, and botanicals clinically proven to support growth.

Key nutrients include Biotin (Vitamin B7) which strengthens keratin—the building block of hair. Vitamin D low levels can slow down the hair cycle. Iron is especially important for women, as low iron levels lead to more shedding. Zinc keeps follicles strong and reduces breakage. Collagen and marine extracts found in Nutrafol promote elasticity and follicle strength.

Our stylists help pair internal supplements like Nutrafol with professional scalp care—because hair health is both an inside and outside job.

What Are the Big 3 for Thinning Hair?

When researching hair loss, you may have heard of the big three: Minoxidil, Finasteride, and Ketoconazole. While effective, they often come with side effects.

At Studio One, we take a more holistic approach: Nutrafol supports growth from within using natural adaptogens and DHT blockers. Oribe Serene Scalp Densifying Line boosts scalp circulation and strengthens follicles with proven growth factors. Professional Beaded Row Extensions add fullness and protect natural strands while hair regenerates.

This trio combines science, self-care, and style—because confidence should grow alongside your hair.

Hair Growth Is a Lifestyle Commitment

Regrowing and thickening hair isn't a quick fix—it's a commitment to daily self-care. With the right combination of products, nutrients, and salon support, real transformation happens over time. At Studio One, we guide you through that journey, one healthy hair day at a time.

Whether you're just noticing thinning or ready to fully restore volume, our team personalizes a growth plan for you.
