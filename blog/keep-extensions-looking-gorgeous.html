How to Keep Your Hair Extensions Looking Gorgeous This Summer—and Beyond!

Cat Barco May 1, 2024

Summer might be officially over, but let's be honest: the pool days, outdoor playdates, and last-minute sun-soaked weekends aren't going anywhere. If you're like many moms, you're still busy keeping the kids entertained while trying to keep your hair extensions looking fabulous. I'm Cat Barco, owner of Studio One Hair Design, and today we're diving into some tips on how to protect your hair extensions from a sneaky little villain—sunscreen. Spoiler alert: it's not just a poolside problem; it's an all-the-time problem.

Sunscreen is our skin's best friend, but for your hair extensions? Not so much. Chemical sunscreens, especially those with Avobenzone, are like that one friend who "accidentally" spills wine on your new white dress—they can turn your gorgeous extensions a not-so-gorgeous shade of orange or pink. And once that happens, there's no amount of hairspray or dry shampoo that's going to save you.

Ever been innocently spraying sunscreen on your kids when suddenly you're caught in a cloud of mist that you swear follows you like a bad ex? Yeah, that's Avobenzone plotting to ruin your extensions. Even your favorite face creams aren't innocent—if they have Avobenzone and your extensions brush up against them, it's like the kiss of death for your hairline.

1. Use Mineral-Based Sunscreens: Don't Let Avobenzone Win. Ditch the chemical culprits. Look for mineral-based sunscreens with zinc oxide or titanium dioxide. They're like the bodyguards your extensions never knew they needed.

2. Shield Your Hair with a Leave-In Conditioner. Think of leave-in conditioner as a superhero cape for your extensions. It adds a protective layer, helping fend off any sneaky sprays of doom trying to land on your luscious locks.

3. Style in Braids or Rock a Hat: Fashion and Function. Want to protect your hair and look stylish doing it? Loose braids and cute hats are your best friends. Plus, a hat gives you the added bonus of hiding from that one mom who won't stop talking about her kale smoothie cleanse.

4. Stay Aware of Your Surroundings: Sunscreen Mists Are the Ninjas of the Pool. If there's one thing we've learned, it's that "sunscreen mist is like glitter—you'll find it everywhere, including on your extensions." Keep an eye out for those rogue sprayers, especially around your blonde strands.

5. Communicate with Friends and Family: A Little PSA Goes a Long Way. Don't be afraid to give your loved ones a gentle heads-up about where they're pointing that sunscreen can. A simple, "Hey, can we aim away from my hair? Thanks!" can save your extensions—and your sanity.

Keeping your extensions looking gorgeous doesn't have to be a summer-only task. By choosing the right products, shielding your hair, and staying alert for those sneaky sunscreen saboteurs, you can keep your investment looking great year-round.
