Local Spotlight: Wassabi on Fire – A Family-Friendly Favorite in Montecito Plaza

Studio One Team April 8, 2024

Just steps away from Studio One Hair Design, Wassabi on Fire is one of our go-to spots in Montecito Plaza, perfect for a quick bite or a family-friendly meal. Whether you're a local, visiting Fresno, or just got your dream hair done at our salon, this spot is a must-visit. From their fun kids' lunchboxes to mouthwatering rolls and Teppanyaki experiences, Wassabi on Fire delivers a dining experience that's as lively as it is delicious.

At Studio One Hair Design, we specialize in providing natural-looking hair extensions in Fresno, and our guests often spend considerable time with us to achieve their dream hair. To make the experience even more enjoyable, we love ordering lunch from Wassabi on Fire for our guests. Imagine sitting back, relaxing, and having your hair transformed while enjoying a delicious meal from one of the best spots in town. It's all part of the luxury service we provide because we believe great hair and great food are a perfect match.

Wassabi on Fire makes dining easy and enjoyable for families, offering adorable kids' lunchboxes that are a hit with our guests. It's the perfect treat for those little ones who are tagging along while mom gets her hair done. The family-friendly atmosphere ensures everyone feels welcome, making it an ideal spot to grab a bite before or after your salon visit.

When I dine at Wassabi on Fire, my go-to order is the Max Roll—it's just that good! But if I'm feeling a little extra, I'll throw in some fried rice. And let me tell you, "the sweet and spicy sauce is non-negotiable." It's funny how my kids won't touch their food without it. I swear it's their secret specialty sauce! Once you try it, you'll understand why it's a must-have with every bite. It's like magic in a bottle, adding the perfect kick to every dish.

One of the highlights at Wassabi on Fire is their Teppanyaki seating. It's not just about the food—it's about the performance. The talented chefs cook right in front of you, turning a meal into a show. With their music, skillful knife work, and flair, it's an experience that makes dining out exciting and memorable. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or just want a fun night out, their Teppanyaki is always a crowd-pleaser.

Wassabi on Fire offers Teppanyaki seating, perfect for groups or family gatherings. For reservations of eight or more, there's a $10 per person deposit that goes onto a gift card, which you can use anytime. Just make sure to book in advance—48 hours' notice is required for any changes to your reservation. To reserve your spot, call 559-797-4361 and get ready for a night of great food and fun!

Wassabi on Fire is more than just a restaurant; it's part of our Montecito Plaza community. Whether you're stopping by for a quick lunchbox for the kids, indulging in the Max Roll with a side of fried rice, or enjoying the Teppanyaki show, it's a dining destination that never disappoints. And if you're with us at Studio One Hair Design, you might just get to enjoy a taste of Wassabi on Fire during your service. Next time you're in the neighborhood—whether for an appointment at Studio One or just exploring Fresno—don't forget to drop by Wassabi on Fire. It's our little slice of sushi heaven right here in the Central Valley.
