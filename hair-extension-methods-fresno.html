Signature Method

Active Genius™ Wefts

Our signature beaded row method for maximum fullness, length, and customization

Active Genius™ Wefts are installed using our signature beaded row method and are the best choice for guests who want the most customized result possible. This method gives us more control over placement, density, and overall blend, which makes it ideal for fuller transformations.

Because the install is built around your specific hair goals, this method works especially well for women who want a bigger change in the way their hair looks and feels.

Best for:

  • women who want maximum fullness
  • women who want more dramatic length
  • guests who want a fully customized install
  • long-term extension wearers
  • thicker hair or more complex blends

Why guests choose this method

The beaded row foundation allows us to customize your result more precisely. That means more flexibility in placement, more control over density, and a fuller finished look.

Maintenance

Move-up appointments are typically every 6 to 8 weeks, depending on your natural hair growth, density, and lifestyle.

Fast & Flexible

No Sew Luxe™ Extensions

A lighter, faster option installed with our exclusive 15-Minute Method™

No Sew Luxe™ Extensions are designed for guests who want beautiful hair with less time and less commitment. This method is lightweight, discreet, and ideal for women who want soft fullness or moderate length without choosing a more customized sewn install.

It is a great option for first-time extension guests and for women who want a seamless result with a lighter feel.

Best for:

  • first-time extension guests
  • women who want a faster install
  • guests with finer hair
  • guests with more sensitive scalps
  • women wanting soft fullness or moderate length
  • guests looking for a lighter, lower-commitment option

Why guests choose this method

No Sew Luxe™ offers speed, comfort, and simplicity. It is a beautiful option for women who want premium hair and a natural-looking blend, but do not need the maximum density or customization of a beaded row install.

Maintenance

A mini move-up can be done around 4 weeks. A full move-up is typically done every 6 to 8 weeks, depending on your hair texture, density, and scalp health. Only one mini move-up is allowed between full move-ups to help protect scalp health.

Which Hair Extension Method Is Right for You?

Both methods create beautiful, seamless results. The right choice depends on how much fullness you want, how customized you want the install to be, and how often you want to maintain it.

Active Genius™ Wefts

Choose this method if you want:

  • more fullness
  • more length
  • more customization
  • a fuller transformation
  • a beaded row foundation with more control over placement and density

No Sew Luxe™

Choose this method if you want:

  • a faster install
  • a lighter feel
  • a more flexible option
  • soft fullness or moderate length
  • a quick, seamless way to wear extensions

The biggest difference comes down to customization. With our beaded row method, we can create more fullness and more dramatic length because the install is built around your specific goals. With No Sew Luxe™, the density is already selected, so it is a beautiful option for speed and simplicity, but it is not the best fit for guests wanting the fullest result possible.
