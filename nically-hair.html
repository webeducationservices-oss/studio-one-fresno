From the Chair

Hair is more than just strands — it's confidence, beauty, and identity.

As a stylist and someone who wears extensions myself, I’ve experienced firsthand the challenges that come with traditional hand-tied hair extensions. The bulkiness, the shedding, the unnatural seams, and the wasted hair that clients were unknowingly paying for—it didn’t sit right with me. That’s why we created the Active Genius Weft—because it’s simply genius.

I knew there had to be a better way. I wanted extensions that felt as natural as they looked, that blended effortlessly, that wouldn’t shed or fall apart with an active lifestyle. Most importantly, I wanted every weft to be intentional—without extra “mustache” hair adding unnecessary weight and cost.

With Nically Hair, you’re not just getting another brand of extensions—you’re getting a solution to all the common frustrations with traditional hand-tied hair. Whether you’re a client looking for the most natural-looking, long-lasting hair or a stylist searching for the best product to create flawless transformations, Nically Hair’s Active Genius Weft delivers.

“Mustache” refers to the short filler hairs found near the weft in most extensions — they add weight and cost without adding wearable length. We remove them, so every inch you pay for is full-length, usable hair.
